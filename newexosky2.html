James Webb Space Telescope

The most powerful space telescope ever built, capable of analyzing exoplanet atmospheres.

TESS Mission

Scanning the entire sky to discover new exoplanets using the transit method.

Nancy Grace Roman

Future space telescope designed to search for exoplanets using gravitational microlensing.

Extremely Large Telescope

A 39-meter telescope that will directly image Earth-like exoplanets.

51 Pegasi b

The first exoplanet discovered orbiting a main-sequence star, heralding the age of exoplanet discovery.

HD 209458 b "Osiris"

Known for its evaporating atmosphere, providing crucial insights into planetary composition.

Kepler-186f

The first Earth-sized planet discovered in the habitable zone of another star.

TRAPPIST-1 System

A remarkable system of seven Earth-sized planets, several of which are in the habitable zone.
